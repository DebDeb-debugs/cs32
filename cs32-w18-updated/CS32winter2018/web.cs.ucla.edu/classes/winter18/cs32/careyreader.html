Lecture 1 Slides and Reader

Carey Nachenberg uses PowerPoint slides when teaching his class. If you'd like to obtain a printed and spiral-bound reader containing those slides (to take notes on and use during our open-book, open-note midterms/final), you can purchase them at Copymat Westwood. The reader costs about $60 plus tax; this is the cost to print and bind the handouts — Carey's making no profit on the readers, so this is as cheap as Copymat can sell them. If that's too expensive for you, he's also posted copies of all of his slides (both animated versions and printable PDF versions). Feel free to download them and print them on your own if you like.

If you'd like to purchase a reader, read Copymat's instructions. In short:

  1. Call Copymat Westwood at 310-824-5276 to check whether they have copies of the CS 32 reader in stock. If they do, you can reserve a copy with your credit card over the phone and then go pick it up at your leisure.
  2. If Copymat doesn't have any copies in stock, you can request that they print one for you, and they'll have it ready for you to pick up within 24 hours.
  3. Once you're ready to pick up your reader, simply go to Copymat Westwood at 10919 Weyburn Ave (west of Westwood Blvd.) and pick up your copy. Their hours are 9am-5pm Monday through Friday.

Again, purchasing a reader is totally optional — feel free to print out the slides on your own if you like (or not!). In past years, the department provided printouts for students each week, but this was becoming too costly ($2000+ in printing costs per quarter!), time-consuming, and wasteful (because of unused copies), so we opted to outsource to an external print shop. Unfortunately for you, you have to pay if you want the slide printouts now. Fortunately for you, all of the slides are now nicely bound with a table of contents, section dividers, etc. We suggest that you look at the slide PDFs online before ordering, to make sure they're useful for you. If so, go ahead and order a copy.

If you have any questions, feel free to email Carey.
